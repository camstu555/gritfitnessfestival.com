OBSTACLE COURSE

EVENT NAME: GRIT MILE

WHAT: The toughest 1 mile you will ever run. Each person will run approximately 1 mile for time while negotiating multiple obstacles (Not sure of the number of obstacles at this time).

WHO: For ages 16 and up

DIVISIONS: Rx Men, Rx Women, Open Men, Open Women

FINALS: Top 5 fastest times for each division will compete in the finals. Which is another mile.

PRIZES: Top 3 male and Top 3 female in the Rx division will get cash prize (TBD based on registration). The Top 3 male and Top 3 female in the OPEN division will get swag bag.

DAY: Sunday

TIME: the event will start around 2pm.
