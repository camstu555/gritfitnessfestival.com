WEIGHTLIFTING MEET

GRIT Fitness Festival has teamed up with Three Ships CrossFit Weightlifting to add a top notch weightlifting meet to our 2019 event. This meet will be sanctioned by USAW and will be open to all age divisions (Youth, Junior, Senior, & Master). Awards will be given based off of Sinclair to the top three athletes in each age division.
