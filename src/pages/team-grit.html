TEAM GRIT

Established in 2010 and has grown into one of the premier 2 and 4-person team functional fitness events in the Mid-Atlantic Region. Over the years athletes from up and down the East Coast have traveled to the Hampton Roads area to participate in this TEST OF FITNESS and COMMUNITY BUILDING event.



TEAM GRIT has multiple divisions in order to give everyone an opportunity to test their fitness. This wide selection accomodates all fitness levels, from the seasoned vet to the beginner athlete and everything inbetween. Below you will find a description for each division.



The workouts are designed to test a teams ability to work together in various combinations. Teams will have to develop a strategy on how to best utilize each team member throughout the day. A teams performance may get tested with Power and/or Olympic Lifting, Endurance, Gymnastics, Plyometrics and other uncommon tasks. These movements will be programmed in a variety of ways and teams will compete against each other to determine who is the BEST TEAM!!
