TEEN GRIT

GRIT FITNESS FESTIVAL has teamed up with CrossFit Cafe to add KID GRIT and TEEN GRIT functional fitness competition:

TEEN GRIT is an individual competition for boys and girls ages 14-15 and 16-17. Girls and boys will compete in their own divisions. Workouts will be similar to the ones programmed during the CrossFit Open. Workouts will test Strength, Power, Speed, Endurance, Agility, Coordination, Flexibility, Accuracy, Stamina and Balance.



Athletes
