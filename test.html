This coming February, we will host 400+ athletes and 1,500+ spectators from the Hampton Roads, Richmond, and North Carolina area, at the 27,000 sq/ft Hampton Roads Convention Center.

Grit Fitness Festival will test individuals and teams in various functional fitness competitions.

Individual Kid 8-13

Individual Teen 14-17

Adult teams of 2

Adult teams of 4

Olympic Weightlifting Competition

Grit's Toughest Mile (OCR)
