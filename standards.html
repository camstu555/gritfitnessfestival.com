Standards of Competition



SCALED DIVISION

Movements you may face:
Olympic Lifts (see chart below)
Wall-Balls
Kettle-bell swings
Box Jumps or Step ups
Push-ups
Knees above hips
Burpees
Single-unders
Jumping pull-ups

Movements you will NOT face:
Rope Climbs
Muscle-ups
Pistols
Hand Stand Push-ups
Hand stand walks
Double-unders
Chest to bar Pull-ups


INTERMEDIATE DIVISON

Movements you may face:
Olympic Lifts (see chart below)
Box Jumps 24/20
Toes-to-bar
Pull-ups
Handstand Push-ups
Double-unders

Movements you will not face:
Muscle-ups
Pistols
Handstand walk

RX DVISION

Movements you may face:
Olympic lifts (see chart below)
Toes-to-bar
Pull-ups

Pistols
Muscle-ups
Handstand push-ups
